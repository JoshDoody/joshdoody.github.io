This free guide will help you answer the question "How much should I be getting paid?" by focusing on three areas:

  • Your market value in your industry—start with a baseline for your skillset and experience in your industry
  • Your market value in your geographic region—narrow your scope to your geographic region and local companies
  • Your market value at your specific company—fine-tune your estimate based on what you know about your specific company

Then you can use that estimate as a foundation to get paid you're worth.
