To get paid what you're worth, you need to know what you're worth.

Start your salary discussions with a strong foundation based on your carefully estimated market value. This simple method focuses on three areas:

  • Your market value in your industry
  • Your market value in your geographic region
  • Your market value at a specific company

With these three data points, you can confidently estimate the market value for your skillset and experience, and use that estimate as a foundation to get paid you're worth.
