How do you answer when a recruiter or hiring manager asks you for your current salary or salary expectations? Do you tell them? Do you lie?

How you answer these questions could literally be worth thousands of dollars, so you need to get it right.

You don't want to disclose this personal information because it will almost always cost you money. But you also don't want to be perceived as difficult or evasive.

So what do you say?

With these word-for-word scripts and email templates, you'll know exactly what to say to avoid sharing your current salary and expected salary while pushing the interview process forward in a tactful way.
