You want to ask for a raise, but how?

Asking for a raise can feel pretty scary, so you keep putting it offer for later.

Even if you could figure out how to ask for a raise, you would still need to figure out so many other things...

  • When should you ask for a raise?
  • Do you even deserve a raise?
  • How much should you ask for?
  • What if they say no?

Get Your Next Raise includes 7 short lessons where I show exactly how to answer those questions and more. Plus it includes worksheets and email templates to walk you through the process of asking for your raise.

Here's what you'll learn in each lesson:

  • Lesson 1: Start now—How Get Your Next Raise will get you un-stuck.
  • Lesson 2: Estimate your market value—The first step to determine how much to ask for.
  • Lesson 3: Set your goal—Now that you know your market value, you can set your goal—your target salary—so you ask for the right amount.
  • Lesson 4: Build your case—Show your boss you deserve a raise.
  • Lesson 5: Put your case in writing—Combine your target salary, accomplishments and accolades into a single email template to make your case for your next raise.
  • Lesson 6: Ask for your raise—How to ask your boss for the raise you deserve and follow up to get a definitive result.
  • Lesson 7: Plan your next steps—Learn exactly how to respond if your boss says no or asks you to wait a little longer.
