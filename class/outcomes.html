What you'll learn in this module

This is a short module about what you'll get out of Get Your Next Raise. We'll start with the three most likely outcomes and start working toward them in the next module.

Here are the three most likely outcomes:

  1. You get your raise
  2. You work with your manager to make a plan to get your raise
  3. You do not get your raise

We'll also look at the email templates that you will use to build and document your case as we go.
