What you'll learn in this lesson

This is a short lesson about what you'll get out of Get Your Next Raise. We'll start with the three most likely outcomes and start working toward them in the next lesson.

Here are the three most likely outcomes:

  1. You get your raise
  2. You work with your manager to make a plan to get your raise
  3. You do not get your raise
