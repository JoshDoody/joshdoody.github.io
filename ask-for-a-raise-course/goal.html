What you'll accomplish in this lesson

You will set your goal for your next raise or promotion. If you're pursuing a raise, you'll set your target salary. If you're pursuing a promotion, you'll set your target job.

Your market value will give you a baseline that you can adjust to set your target salary. You'll do that in the next lesson.

Action items

  • Set your target salary
  • Record your target salary in the Get Your Next Raise worksheet
